profile

biography & academic background

I am Zhenjie Zhang (Tankou Kyotsuki). I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science within the Excellent Computer Science Class at Nankai University in June 2026 (having completed all course and graduation thesis requirements early in my Junior year, one year ahead of schedule). Currently, I am heading to Fudan University & Shanghai Innovation Institute (SII) to pursue my Ph.D.

My research interests lie in the intersection of interpretable machine learning models (XAI), bioinformatics, genomics, fMRI analysis, and recommendation systems. Over the course of my studies, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with several labs as a research attachment.
